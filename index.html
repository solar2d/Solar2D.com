Make Your Games Shine

Solar2D is an open-source, production-ready, Lua based 2D game engine, with a focus on rapid development.
Get instant feedback with Live Builds, and publish to every major mobile and desktop platform with ease.

Why Solar2D?
Rapid Iterations
See your changes instantly on desktop with the Solar2D Simulator, or directly on your device with Live Builds.
Vibrant Ecosystem
Hundreds of plugins allow you to integrate Ad Networks and countless features not provided by the core engine. Or easily create your own!
Production Ready
Solar2D is official fork of Corona SDK, which has been in active development for over 10 years, and used by hundreds of thousands of developers to create profitable apps and games.
Batteries Included
Use thousands of built-in APIs in the core engine or add your own with easy native extensions.
Active Community
Join an engaged community of thousands of developers using Solar2D on the Forums and Slack.
Open Source
All source code and resources are available under the MIT license.
Dedicated Development
This is a fully open sourced project that is a fork of the well established and widely used Corona SDK game engine, which is no longer commercially supported. Development is lead by Vlad Shcherban, former technical lead engineer at Corona Labs Inc. If you are using this engine, consider supporting its development.
